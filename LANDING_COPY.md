## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
Yes. No credit card, no login, no trial. You enter your tools and get your audit instantly on screen. We only ask for your email if you want the report sent to you — after you've already seen the results.

**Q: How do you make money if this is free?**
Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — the same tools you're already paying for, at 20–40% off retail. If your audit shows significant savings opportunities, we'll let you know how Credex credits can help. You're never pressured.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
Every number is verified against official vendor pricing pages and updated weekly. We cite every source in our public PRICING_DATA.md. If you find an error, email us and we'll fix it within 24 hours.

**Q: Do you store my spending data?**
We store your audit results so your shareable link works. If you submit your email, we store that too. We don't sell your data. Your specific spend numbers are never shown in the public shareable version of your audit — only the tool names and savings amounts.

**Q: Can I share my audit results?**
Yes — every audit gets a unique public URL you can share with your team or manager. Identifying details (email, company name) are stripped from the public version. Only tool names and savings numbers are shown.
