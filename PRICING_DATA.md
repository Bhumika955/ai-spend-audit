## Notes on enterprise pricing
For tools with custom enterprise pricing (Cursor Enterprise, Claude Enterprise, ChatGPT Enterprise), the audit engine uses the published Business/Team tier as the floor estimate. This is conservative — actual enterprise pricing is typically higher, meaning real savings opportunities are understated, not overstated. This is intentional: we'd rather show conservative savings and be right than show inflated numbers and lose user trust.
